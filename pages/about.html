About Storyteller Studios

Storyteller Studios was started in October 2019 by Kurt Reynolds and Gabe DeGrazia. Our first film was a short called Dating Games. From there we continued to make new short films on a regular basis. As time went on we got better filming equipment and continued to develop our skills on filming, directing, editing, color grading, sound, and acting. We've worked with a lot of great talent in the Louisville, KY area who have helped create some fun and exciting stories.
